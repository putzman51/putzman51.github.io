about

My quiet contribution to a noisy place.

However you hear, read, or understand these writings I only ask that you do so with a spirit of love + care for yourself and others.

These poems + thoughts are open source. Please enjoy, print + share without restrictions.

Keep a pen handy. Have lots of paper around. Extras. Of both. Everywhere.

Thank you for joining me in slow mornings and an even slower enjoyment of the written word.
